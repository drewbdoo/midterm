Badger Creek Soaps

I'm not affiliated whatsoever with Badger Creek Soaps, but I love their products and felt it would be a fun starting point for this project. Their owner, Kegan, is a pretty decent fellow. Here is a bit of their story.

Over a Decade ago I had a dream. It was to provide products that are handmade so that my friends and family could feel good about themselves when they take a shower or bath. I am the creator of Badger Creek, and I am often asked, “Where is Badger Creek?” Well, Badger Creek is not really a place on the map, yet! But a concept at heart and a goal in business and in life. My passion is bringing hand-crafted excellence to all of our products, and creating new and imaginative ideas so our customers can enjoy them. I want to provide an experience for your nose, and share them with you! You can find me at many Renaissance fairs across the USA. We enjoy time spent with our customers and learn as to what works best and what they would like to see from our company and in turn, it helps us grow together! You can also find me at these other websites: Visit our Facebook, YouTube. and my Wholesale site Badger Creek can be found traveling the United States and producing soap at the Arizona Renaissance Festival, the California Pleasure Faire and we have a new shop at the Bristol Renaissance Festival and I can also be found in the Carolina Renaissance Festival. Badger Creek Soap began gaining popularity as more people discovered how great our products perform! adding our mix of products to the Fair’s marketplace. From small beginnings, Badger Creek has grown into a prosperous business. I love what I do!
